Hybrid Livestreaming

A paywall livestream system for The Spectator

I designed and delivered a hybrid live-streaming system that operates simultaneously in front of and behind The Spectator and UnHerd's paywall.

Events broadcast to both public platforms and subscribers at the same time. The free stream stays fully open for an initial period — then automatically switches to a blurred feed with pre-recorded prompts directing viewers to subscribe, while the subscriber stream continues uninterrupted. Highly effective at converting live audiences into paying subscribers.

Broadcast & Webinars

Building a studio for the digital age

At the start of the pandemic, Policy Exchange faced the challenge of moving its entire events programme online without sacrificing quality or brand consistency.

I designed and built a bespoke studio allowing physical and virtual panellists to be mixed into a single live broadcast and Zoom webinar — so audiences could still ask questions and feel part of the event. All sessions are also live-streamed across social platforms.

This was later developed into a full hybrid studio within a new dedicated events space.
